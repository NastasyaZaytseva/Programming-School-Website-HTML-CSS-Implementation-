Criticism at work is your friend, but not always

When you do a code review, sometimes you meet with the fact that you give constructive criticism and it is perceived with hostility, as if it is a personal insult. A person tries to resist feedback with all his might and is not ready to listen to arguments. There are two options here: either the feedback is given incorrectly, or the person does not perceive criticism. In this post, I want to focus on how to work with criticism.
